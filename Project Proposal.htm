Project Objective

The objective of this project is to demonstrate the design of a website that provides data regarding some of my favorite Asian Restaurants in South Miami and my personal reviews of them. This website will target lovers of Asian Food in the South Miami area and inform them regarding some of the places that I enjoy the most.
Each page of this website will be specifically about one Restaurant. Each of these pages will provide the location, recommended dishes, and a short description/review of each Restaurant.There will also be links to the available online resources these restaurants may have. Each page may also have images of food that each restaurant has or an online menu for costumers to look at before going to or ordering from the restaurant.


Format:

Name of Restaurant

Description of Restaurant

Available resources restaurant may have: Pictures Online Menu
