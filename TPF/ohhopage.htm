About Ohho Noodles

Ohho Noodles is a restaurant right off of US1 near the University of Miami Area in Coral Gables. This is my personal favorite restaurant but it is not exclusively a dim sum place. Ohho describes itself as an Asian fusion eatery with Thai and Japanese dishes. The atmosphere has a very inviting urban millenial vibe to it. I have tried all types of dishes there and am yet to have an unpleasant experience. It also serves as a bit of an Asian market as it has authentic imported Asian goods such as ramune. It is a bit on the pricey side in comparison to other Asian restaurants in Miami but the meal will be very much worth it.


Location

1100 S Dixie Hwy,
Coral Gables, FL 33146


Recommended Dishes

Galbi Udon:

My overall favorite dish. Some Galbi Korean Short Ribs with Udon noodles make for the perfect combination.


Pad Thai


Best Pad Thai I have had in Miami. It has the perfect sweet and savory fusion and is a huge hit if you
enjoy the dish.


Lo Mein


Chicken and Beef Lo meein are very good, just depends on your preferance. Noodles in the lo mein are
full of tast in every bite.


Beef Bibimbap


One of my favorite dishes at Ohho. Hot, sweet, and sour all in one, a must have.


Any order of Gyozas or Dumplings


All dimsum is served fresh and warm as soon as it is made. My favorite is the pork gyoza.
