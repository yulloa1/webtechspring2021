Komodo Miami


About Komodo

Komodo is a three-story establishment that has both a restaurant that serves contemporary Southeast Asian cuisine and a lounge with a wonderful assortment of cocktails and drinks. It is located right off of Brickell Ave in the heart of Miami's Brickell neighborhood. Komodo has some beautiful trendy decor that has caused it to become a celebrity hotspot but it is very pricey so be warned. Nonetheless it is a great place to go when celebrating an event such as an anniversary or a birthday and it still has high quality great tasting Asian food.


Location

801 Brickell Ave,
Miami, FL 33131


Recommended Dishes

Tuna Toro roll


Tuna Toro roll is delicious, the tuna tastes unbelievably fresh.


Salmon Truffle Honey roll


The salmon truffle honey roll is probably my favorite roll here. The truffle
fusion with the salmon makes for an great combination.


Peking Duck


The duck is a modern take on the classic chinese dish. It is cooked to perfection
as it is well known for its crispyness.


Calamari Salad


If you are a fan of calamari and also salads, then this is the dish for you.


Wagyu Tacos


A bit on the pricier side, but definetely a dish that is worth it if you are
if your willing to splurge.


Lobster Dynamite roll


One of my favorite rolls here. If you are a fan of lobster than you will enjoy
this fried roll.
