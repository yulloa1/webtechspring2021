Novikov Miami


About Novikov

Novikov is an up scale Chinese-Japanese fusion restaurant on in downtown Miami right off Brickell Ave. It has both inside and outdoor seating with a beautiful terrace that overlooks Biscayne Bay. Novikov has a modern decor with an open kitchen, and delicious upmarket sushi and seafood dishes. It is unfortunately quite pricey so it might be a good destination to go on a date or celebrate an event. Nonetheless it has wonderful dim sum and sushi options and creative cocktails so if you don't mind the price tag, it is a great destination to eat at.


Location

300 South Biscayne Boulevard
Miami, FL 33131


Recommended Dishes

Duck and Foie Gras Dumplings


These dumplings are part of Novikovs dim sum carts that patrol the restaurant.
They are my favorite dish from the dim sum cart.


King Crab Crispy


These king crab legs are best known for their presenation. A huge crab leg in
all of its grandeur, a great option for lovers of crab.


Peking Duck


The peking duck is made delicious. It is well known for having the perfect ratio
of crispiness.


Yuzu Miso Black Cod


This Miso marinated black cod is one of the biggest hits with lovers of fish.
If you are ordering an entree instead of ordering dishes to share with a group,
I highly reccommend this dish.


Yellowtail Cilantro Sashimi


This sashimi roll is incredibly refreshing and full of taste. Lovers of sashimi
will absolutely love this roll.
