About Ohho Noodles

Ohho Noodles is a restaurant right off of US1 near the University of Miami Area in Coral Gables. This is my personal favorite restaurant but it is not exclusively a dim sum place. Ohho describes itself as an Asian fusion eatery with Thai and Japanese dishes. I have tried all types of dishes there and am yet to have an unpleasant experience. It also serves as a bit of an Asian market as it has authentic imported Asian goods such as ramune. It is a bit on the pricey side in comparison to other Asian restaurants in Miami but the meal will be very much worth it.
