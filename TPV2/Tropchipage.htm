Tropical Chinese


About Tropical Chinese

Tropical Chinese is a Dim Sum restaurant located right off of Bird Road next to Tropical Park in South Miami. This restaurant is great to go to with big groups to share all the food served. Tropical Chinese serves Hong Kong Style push cart Dim Sum that is all handcrafted in their kitchen. This is probably the restaurant with the best reputation as it serves high quality authentic Dim Sum at a very reasonable price.


Location

7991 SW 40th Street
Miami, FL 33155


Recommended Dishes

Shangai Pork Bun


Delicious warm soup dimsum that just explodes in your mouth as soon as you bite into it.
My personal favorite food to order here.


Pork Siumai


You cannot go wrong with some Siumai, as the tradiotional chinese dumplings are a hit in
this eatery.


Peking Duck


The Peking Duck may just be some of the best duck I have ever had, and for the fraction of
the price of a standard place. Servers also serve the duck prewrapped to the customers.


Shrimp Dumpling


If you are a fan of shrimp, then the you will love the shrimp dumpling.


Baked Pork Bun


Baked Pork Buns are highly recomendable to anyone that enjoys buns, as they come out nice
and warm and are as authentic as they come.


Fried Shrimp Wonton


This deep fried dish may not be the healthiest alternative, but it certainly does taste great.
